Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 30, 2026

GroceryRun ("we", "our", "the app") is a free grocery deal aggregator available at groceryrun.shop. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

What Data We Collect

GroceryRun does not require an account and does not collect personal information on our servers. All data is stored locally on your device:

  • Zip code — saved in your browser's localStorage so you don't have to re-enter it each visit.
  • Grocery list — items you add to your list are saved in localStorage on your device.
  • Address cache — store location data is cached locally to speed up future searches.

Location Data

If you allow it, the app may use:

  • Browser geolocation — your device's GPS/location to find your zip code. This data is processed locally and not sent to our servers.
  • IP-based geolocation — on first visit, we may use ipapi.co to estimate your location from your IP address. This request goes directly from your browser to ipapi.co. We do not store your IP address.

Third-Party Services

The app communicates with the following third-party services directly from your browser:

  • Flipp (backflipp.wishabi.com) — provides grocery circular and deal data. Subject to Flipp's Privacy Policy.
  • OpenStreetMap / Nominatim — provides store location data. Subject to OSM's Privacy Policy.
  • ipapi.co — provides IP-based location estimation. Subject to ipapi.co's Privacy Policy.
  • GitHub Pages — hosts the app. GitHub may collect standard server logs (IP address, browser info). See GitHub's Privacy Statement.

Shared Lists

When you share a grocery list, the list data is encoded in the URL itself (not stored on any server). Anyone with the link can view the list contents. Shared links do not contain your location, identity, or any personal information.

Cookies

GroceryRun does not set cookies. We use localStorage, which stays on your device and is not transmitted to any server.

Data Retention & Deletion

All data is stored in your browser's localStorage. You can delete it at any time by:

  • Clearing your browser's site data for groceryrun.shop
  • Using your browser's "Clear browsing data" feature

We have no server-side data to delete because we don't store any.

Children's Privacy

GroceryRun is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue at our GitHub repository.
